Our Story

In the bustling city of Bhagalpur, Manoj Mahto, a software engineer with a knack for solving complex problems, and Puja Panchal, a passionate journalist with a love for storytelling, crossed paths at a tech conference where Manoj was presenting a groundbreaking project. Puja was there to cover the event, and as she interviewed Manoj, their conversation flowed effortlessly, sparking an instant connection. Despite their different fields, they discovered a shared curiosity and love for exploring new ideas. Over cups of coffee and long conversations, their friendship blossomed into love. Manoj admired Puja's ability to find stories that mattered, while Puja appreciated Manoj's analytical mind and supportive nature. One starry night, Manoj surprised Puja with a heartfelt proposal, promising to be her partner in all of life's adventures. With tears of joy, she accepted, knowing she had found her perfect match. Join us in celebrating the union of Manoj and Puja as they embark on a new chapter filled with love, laughter, and shared dreams. Together, we can create moments that will last a lifetime.
